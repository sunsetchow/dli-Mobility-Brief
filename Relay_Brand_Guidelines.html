Brand Essence

We make the school run disappear.

Relay is a supervised autonomous vehicle service that handles the school run for single parents in outer London — so every parent, regardless of income, disability or shift pattern, can trust that their child is safely at school while they reclaim the hours they need to work, rest, and build a better life.

Trustworthy Safety-first, every journey
Warm Human care, not just tech
Inclusive Designed for those overlooked
Reliable Consistent, predictable, calm

Logo & Wordmark

RELAY

Supervised AV School Transport

Primary — use on light backgrounds

RELAY

Supervised AV School Transport

Reversed — use on dark backgrounds

Do maintain clear space equal to the height of the "R" on all sides of the wordmark.

Do always include the coral dot — it represents the handover, the core of the Relay concept.

Don't stretch, recolour, or apply drop shadows to the wordmark. Use only approved colour pairs.

Don't display the wordmark below 120px / 1" wide — it loses legibility at smaller sizes.

Voice & Tone

How Relay speaks

Calm & Confident

DO

"Your children are on their way. Chaperone Sarah is with them."

DON'T

"Don't worry! Everything is totally fine!"

Clear, Not Clinical

DO

"Book once. Relay handles the rest."

DON'T

"Utilise our platform for optimised transport logistics."

Empowering

DO

"You can make it to your 8am shift. We've got the school run."

DON'T

"We solve the problem for parents who can't manage."

Human First

DO

"Sarah remembers your son likes to sit by the window."

DON'T

"Our AI-optimised chaperone system ensures passenger compliance."

Imagery & Iconography

✓  Do
  • Real families from outer London — diverse, authentic, unstaged
  • Candid moments of children being safely dropped off or picked up
  • Warm natural light — morning school run, after-school golden hour
  • Close-up shots of handover moments: chaperone → child → teacher
  • Smartphone screens showing the live tracking app, shown in context
  • Represent disability and neurodiverse families naturally and positively
✗  Don't
  • Stock photo smiles, aspirational lifestyle imagery
  • Cold blue tech shots of autonomous vehicles in empty streets
  • Imagery that emphasises poverty, hardship, or helplessness
  • Images without representation of disability or diverse families
  • Posed "parent and child" shots that feel generic or corporate

Iconography

Filled circle style

Monoline or solid-fill. No gradients.

Consistent weight

2px stroke at 24px. Scale proportionally.

Relay Teal or Deep

Light bg → navy. Dark bg → cream or sky.

Always labelled

Pair icons with labels. Never use alone in CTAs.
